Lord Buddha Educational Foundation Society

Through various initiatives such as educational programs, seminars, workshops, and publications, the Lord Buddha Educational Foundation Society endeavors to empower individuals with knowledge that can lead to personal growth and societal harmony. By emphasizing values such as compassion, wisdom, and mindfulness, the foundation seeks to contribute to the betterment of humanity and the world at large.

OUR STORY

We are the Lord Buddha Educational Foundation Society, a dedicated group of individuals united by our profound reverence for the teachings of Lord Buddha. Rooted in the timeless wisdom of Buddhism, we are committed to fostering education, enlightenment, and compassion in the world.

Our vision at the Lord Buddha Educational Foundation Society is to cultivate a world where the timeless teachings of Lord Buddha serve as a guiding light for all humanity. We envision a society where compassion, wisdom, and mindfulness are cherished values, guiding individuals and communities towards harmony and enlightenment.
